SLAVE ONE

Slave One is a space simulation game based on Star Wars. The players take the control of Boba Fett's spaceship, Slave One, and fight against the empire to destroy their last annihilation weapon.

The game it was made by scratch in our own engine. The whole class worked in this univesity project.

From the beginning of this project I have been working in the desing aspects of the game. I worked above all in the cockpit of the Slave I, apart of taking gameplay decisions and exploring the Star Wars world in order to enrich my decisions.
