ABOUT ME

Hello! I am a game developer living in Barcelona looking for a Level Design position. I studied Design and Development of Video Games Bachelor's Degree in the UPC (Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya). I spent the last semester of the degree in Glasgow focusing my studies in Game Design and Level Design at GCU (Glasgow Caledonian University).

I started playing videogames on the ps1 when I was 3 years old, since then I knew I wanted to spent my life around them. I am sociable active person ready to new challenges! Always open to get feedback and very commmunicative. Let's work together!

On my free time I really like to travel, I always take my camera with me and record videos which I later edit with Adobe Premiere. Music is also my passion, I play the piano and I can spend hours looking for the perfect song to fit in my videos! And finally I love basketball, I can't remember the first time I started shooting so hit me up if you are up to some game!
