Post Apocalyptic Environments

Final Project

Over the course of a year, I conducted an academic research project and developed a prototype about "Methodologies to create post-apocalyptic environments based on real cities". The main objective was to compile in a document the whole process of how to turn real scenarios into virtual ones in a post apocalyptic setting in order to help small companies that have small budget.

After the research, I jumped into the development of the prototype. I was in charge of all the programming and design tasks, since I worked with an artist. If you want to know more about the project you can check the full work I did HERE .
